Who Am I?

My name is Adam Green. I grew up in a small town named Vassalboro here in Maine. I went to Vassalboro Community School, a k-8 school, for nine years and then chose to go to Winslow High School, where I got my diploma. I had planned to come into the University for computer engineering, but after my first day of classes I knew that it wasn't the right fit for me. I made the switch to business, and I think I have made the right choice.
